Privacy Policy for QuakeBot

Effective Date: April 2025

Thank you for using QuakeBot. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our bot and services.

1. Information Collection

QuakeBot does not collect or store any personal data from users. We do not track your location, access your contacts, or require login credentials.

2. Facebook & Telegram Integration

We use Facebook Graph API to post public earthquake updates to a Facebook Page. No user data is accessed or stored from Facebook accounts. On Telegram, the bot reads public channel settings to send updates. No private Telegram data is accessed.

3. Earthquake Data

All data shown in the bot is pulled from publicly available earthquake feeds such as USGS, EMSC, and the Thai Meteorological Department.

4. Data Security

QuakeBot runs in a secure environment and does not process or log sensitive user data. No external databases are used to store user information.

5. Changes to Policy

We may update this privacy policy in the future. All changes will be reflected on this page with a new effective date.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact the developer at myanmarquakebot@gmail.com.

By using this service, you agree to this privacy policy.
